Introduction

MY WORK LOG

From September to October, I learned a lot about design. In addition to SAE lessons, I read books and searched the Internet for useful information and tutorial videos, and tried simple tasks. During this time I came across many interesting project ideas. It was not easy to pick one to try as an exam project. My first idea was to create a website for a restaurant, in the end I felt it was not close to my heart. I wanted to make a homepage for:

- a bookstore with an owl logo

- a webshop selling craft soaps

- a vintage style interior decorator

- a downtown coffee shop with a coffee and a croissant logo

- a smart home real estate agency

- a private kindergarten with a pet logo

- a meditation center
